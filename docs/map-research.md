# Awaji–Tokushima map references

The game deliberately uses a stylized node-like region rather than real geography. These web references informed the distinct visual/mechanical beats, while all map and monster art remains original:

- [Tokushima Prefecture tourism (AwaNavi)](https://www.awanavi.jp/en/) — Tokushima hub and Awa Odori-inspired warm lantern palette.
- [Nijigen no Mori](https://nijigennomori.com/en/) — Awaji theme-park/safari framing for a later collectible area.
- [Japan National Tourism Organization](https://www.japan.travel/en/) — regional landmark research starting point; Naruto whirlpools and Mt. Bizan are represented as a water route and mountain lookout instead of literal geographic reconstructions.

Current playable map: the Awa Gate hub, Health House, grass route, Mt. Bizan silhouette, and Naruto Sea edge. The landmark labels establish the expansion paths without shipping their later-area asset packs in the boot payload.
